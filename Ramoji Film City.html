Ramoji Film City

Ramoji Film City, located in Hyderabad, is recognized as the world’s largest integrated film studio complex, covering over 2,000 acres. Established in 1991 by Ramoji Rao, it serves as a hub for filmmakers from across the globe, offering world-class facilities for movie production, pre-production, and post-production. This massive film city is not just a working studio but also a popular tourist destination, attracting millions of visitors each year. Inside the film city, visitors can explore numerous film sets that replicate famous landmarks, urban streets, royal palaces, gardens, airports, and even entire villages. It’s like stepping into different worlds without leaving Hyderabad. The themed attractions, such as the Japanese Garden, Mughal Gardens, and Hawa Mahal set, provide endless photo opportunities. Guided tours take you behind the scenes to witness the magic of filmmaking, from shooting floors to editing rooms. Ramoji Film City also offers a range of entertainment shows and activities for visitors of all ages. These include stunt shows, dance performances, and adventure activities such as zorbing, archery, and a thrilling amusement park. The complex features multiple hotels, restaurants, and food courts, ensuring a comfortable stay for tourists who want to explore it over multiple days. Recognized by the Guinness World Records as the largest film studio complex in the world, Ramoji Film City is more than just a movie-making hub — it’s a place where imagination comes to life. Whether you’re a cinema lover, a family on vacation, or an adventure seeker, Ramoji Film City offers a unique blend of entertainment, culture, and creativity that makes it a must-visit destination in Hyderabad.
